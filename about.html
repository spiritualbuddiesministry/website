About The Ministry

A community built to grow in truth and reflect Christ with compassion.

Spiritual Buddies Ministry is a group of Adventist believers established purposely to learn the Word of God and collaborate in different social issues. Our heartbeat is to see faith become visible in worship, character, fellowship, and practical service.

Our Story

Learning the Word of God together, then carrying it into daily life.

The ministry was formed with a simple but powerful purpose: to gather believers around Scripture and build a Christ-honoring community where spiritual growth and social responsibility move hand in hand.

Our fellowship values both biblical depth and practical love. We believe Christian faith should not remain abstract; it should transform conversation, choices, service, and relationships.

V

Vision

To be a spiritually grounded Adventist community where believers grow in truth and become a blessing to society.

M

Mission

To learn the Word of God faithfully, strengthen one another in fellowship, and collaborate in addressing social issues with compassion.

C

Core Character

Reverence, unity, kindness, biblical conviction, servant-hearted leadership, and practical ministry.

What Guides Us

Values that shape the culture of the ministry.

Scripture First

We want every teaching, conversation, and decision to be anchored in the Word of God.

Christlike Fellowship

We create a welcoming atmosphere where encouragement, accountability, and grace can flourish.

Prayerful Dependence

We believe ministry becomes fruitful when it is carried by prayer and surrendered to God.

Compassionate Action

We care about the social realities around us and seek practical ways to respond as the body of Christ.
